## üìä Understanding BMI

BMI is a simple way to estimate if your weight is healthy for your height. It uses a formula:

```
BMI = weight (kg) / (height (m) √ó height (m))
```

When you use the app with metric units, it does this calculation for you. If you use imperial units, the app converts your info to metric behind the scenes.

Here are the BMI categories:

| Category    | BMI Range   |
|-------------|-------------|
| Underweight | Less than 18.5  |
| Normal      | 18.5 to 24.9    |
| Overweight  | 25 to 29.9      |
| Obese       | 30 or higher    |

Knowing your BMI helps you understand if you might need to gain or lose weight, but it doesn‚Äôt tell you everything about your health. Always ask a doctor for health advice.
